Sync Lyrics

This tool will help you create a file syncing a video with its lyrics

Step 1

It should look something like https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oHRNrgDIJfo
You can easily find it on specialized sites like genius.com

Step 2

As the song plays, click on the word that corresponds to what is being sung. This will place a "keyframe" at that spot. The more keyframes you add, the more precise the lyrics syncing will be. You can freely add keyframes: you can even come travel "back" in the lyrics, which is usually useful for the chorus section.

You can freely pause and move the video around if needed. You may also slow down the playback speed to increase your precision.
